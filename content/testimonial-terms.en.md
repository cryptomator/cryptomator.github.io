## Information on the Optional Use of Your Testimonial

### 1. Voluntary Consent

Giving consent to the use of your testimonial is entirely voluntary and is not part of the sweepstakes.

Your decision has no effect on your participation, your chances of winning, or any prize notification.

### 2. What This Consent Covers

If you give your consent, Skymatic GmbH may use the testimonial you submitted on the previous page together with your name, job title, and company as a testimonial for Cryptomator.

If necessary, we may also contact you in relation to your testimonial, for example to clarify details.

### 3. Where Your Testimonial May Be Used

Your testimonial may be published on the Cryptomator website and used in presentations, marketing materials, and social media posts by Skymatic.

### 4. Legal Basis

The legal basis for this processing is your consent pursuant to Art. 6(1)(a) GDPR.

### 5. Withdrawal of Consent

You may withdraw your consent at any time with effect for the future by contacting us at privacy [at] cryptomator [dot] org.

Withdrawal of consent does not affect your participation in the sweepstakes or your chances of winning.

### 6. Further Information

Further information on how we process personal data can be found in our [Privacy Policy](/en/privacy/).
