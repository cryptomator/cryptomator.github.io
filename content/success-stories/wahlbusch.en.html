The solution was implemented across departments - from the IT department to the works council and executive management. A practical example: At Walbusch, function-based vaults are used that are provided to employees as network drives. This makes the additional security layer virtually invisible in daily work, as access to data is possible without detours after unlocking the vaults. Only when opening very large files can slightly longer loading times occasionally occur. Overall, Cryptomator Hub integrates seamlessly into the existing system environment and supports employees without significant restrictions. "For our employees, it was crucial that the security effort in daily operations remains low - exactly what Cryptomator Hub offers us." - title: "Solution for Walbusch" anchor: "walbusch-solution" content: | The implementation process gave Walbusch the opportunity to specifically modernize existing security processes. The data previously encrypted in Boxcryptor was successfully and reliably transferred to Cryptomator Hub. The onboarding process was also structured and smooth: When unlocking vaults for the first time, IT and specialized departments supported hand in hand - a collaboration that made the transition particularly efficient. Furthermore, Cryptomator Hub significantly contributed to overcoming existing challenges in data security. Particularly appreciated is the combination of end-to-end encryption and role-based permission management. This ensures sensitive data is reliably protected while clear access rights simplify internal processes.
